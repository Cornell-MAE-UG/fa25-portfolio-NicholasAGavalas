MAE 3780 Final project 


For MAE 3780 We were instructed to design a robot to compete in a cube collecting competition. Our robot was named the sticky bandit and featured a custom acrylic frame and a color sensor to detect color changes. Our mantra was to do basic well, where the robot would enter the center of the board serpentine around and then spiral. We implemented a custom PWM drive function to make this easier.

   
The robot preformed okay, and by okay I mean it maintained a neutral record. It struggled in the light detecting the color change of the yellow to blue and would frequently get bullied by other heavier robots.
